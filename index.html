Mobile Ticket Application

Conventional Ticket Replacement

This mobile application was intended to replace the traditional ticketing system in place for J2 Acid Solutions LLC. The original paper ticketing system in place involved a lot of redundant back and forth between the field hand, office, and buyer. This made the paper tickets highly prone to field hand errors. This ticketing system minimized user error by syncing with a online database and limiting what information the field hand could change.

Tools Used

  • Ionic 4
  • Angular 10
  • Google Firebase
